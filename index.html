Banff

Banff is a natural wilderness area in southwestern Alberta, Canada. It's along the eastern slopes of the Rocky Mountains and abuts the border with British Columbia. The rest of the land surrounding the park is within provincial parks or other protected areas. Banff is noted for its beauty, plant and animal diversity, and other processes.
